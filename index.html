Our Story

Growing Wellness with Natural and Organic Excellence

Abha Estate is an exquisite organic farm located near Jaipur, designed to offer a holistic farm-to-table experience. At the heart of the estate are the revered Gir cows, known for their high-quality milk.

These cows are nurtured on a diet of organic feed, ensuring that the milk is pure and rich in nutrients. The milk is then used to produce traditional paneer and desi ghee, both staples of the Indian kitchen, made with the utmost care and authenticity.
